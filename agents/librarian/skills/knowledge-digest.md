# SKILL: knowledge-digest

## Trigger
Weekly digest, resurface knowledge, stale notes, review saved resources

## Steps
1. Pick the notes that have been surfaced the fewest times (oldest first on ties)
2. Lead each entry with the note title, then its pending next actions
3. Keep the whole digest scannable in under a minute

## Output Format
- Max 3 notes per digest
- Only unchecked next actions are shown
- End with nothing — no sign-off, no filler
